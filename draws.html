Draw Mechanics

How the Draw Works

01

Log Your Scores

Enter your last 5 Stableford scores (1–45). These become your draw numbers. Adding a new score replaces the oldest one automatically.

02

Monthly Draw

At the end of each month, 5 numbers between 1-45 are drawn. We use either random lottery mode or algorithmic mode weighted by score popularity.

03

Win Prizes

Match 3 numbers = 25% share. Match 4 numbers = 35% share. Match all 5 = 40% jackpot. Prizes split among winners. Jackpot rolls over if no 5-match winner.
