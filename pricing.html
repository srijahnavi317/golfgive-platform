FAQ

Common Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! Cancel from your Dashboard → Subscription tab at any time. Access continues until the end of the current billing period. No penalties or hidden fees.

How do Stripe payments work?

We use Stripe — the world's leading payments platform — to handle all transactions. Your card details are never stored on our servers. Stripe uses bank-level 256-bit encryption. You can pay with Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and more.

How do I enter the monthly draw?

Log your Stableford scores (1–45) in your dashboard. Your 5 most recent scores become your draw numbers automatically. Active subscribers are entered into every monthly draw.

What happens if my payment fails?

Stripe will automatically retry failed payments. If all retries fail, your subscription will be paused and we'll email you. You can update your payment method from the billing portal anytime to restore access immediately.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly?

Yes! From your Dashboard → Subscription tab, click "Manage Subscription" to access the Stripe portal where you can switch plans, update payment methods, or download invoices.

What percentage goes to charity?

You choose between 10% and 50% of your subscription. Set this during signup and adjust anytime from your dashboard.
