How GolfGive Works

Three simple steps between you and your next win — while making a real difference in the world.

01

Subscribe & Choose Your Charity

Sign up for a monthly or yearly plan. Choose which charity your contribution supports. You choose the percentage — minimum 10%.

Monthly from £9.99 Yearly saves 25%
02

Log Your Golf Scores

Enter your last 5 Stableford scores (1–45). Your numbers become your draw entries. The more you play, the stronger your entries.

03

Win Prizes Every Month

Monthly draws with 3-match, 4-match and 5-match prizes. Jackpot rolls over until someone wins. Real cash prizes paid directly to winners.
